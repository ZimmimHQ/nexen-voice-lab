# NEXEN AI Voice Lab - Voice Blend Documentation

## Overview

Professional AI-powered voice synthesis and blending studio. Create natural, expressive voiceovers by blending two distinct voices with precise control over tone, tempo, and character.

---

## Features

- Dual voice blending with adjustable ratio (0% to 100%)
- 60+ premium voices across multiple languages
- Real-time voice preview before generation
- Speed control (0.5x to 2.0x)
- Multi-language support (English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Chinese)
- High-fidelity 44.1kHz WAV output
- Batch processing for long texts
- Progress tracking with real-time logs

---

# How to Use

## Step 1: Set Your Project Title

1. Enter a descriptive name for your audio file in the **Project Title** field.
2. This will be the filename when you download your generated audio.
3. Example: `Corporate_Narration_V1` or `Podcast_Intro_2024`.

## Step 2: Write or Paste Your Script

1. Enter the text you want to convert to speech in the **Script Content** area.
2. The system automatically splits long texts into manageable chunks.
3. Maximum length is approximately **5,000 characters** per generation.
4. For longer content, generate multiple sections and combine them later.

## Step 3: Select Your Voices

- Choose a Primary Voice.
- Choose a Secondary Voice.
- Use Preview to hear samples before generating.

## Step 4: Adjust Voice Blend Ratio

| Value | Result |
|---------|---------|
| 0.0 | Pure Primary Voice |
| 0.5 | Equal Blend |
| 1.0 | Pure Secondary Voice |

## Step 5: Set Language and Speed

Select the appropriate language and speaking tempo.

## Step 6: Generate Audio

1. Click **GENERATE NEXEN VOICE**.
2. Monitor progress through the Process Timer.
3. Review logs and progress updates.
4. Cancel if needed.

## Step 7: Download Your Audio

1. Listen to the generated audio.
2. Click Download.
3. Save the WAV file.

# Frequently Asked Questions

## How long can my text be?

Approximately 5,000 characters per generation.

## What audio format is generated?

WAV, 44.1kHz, 16-bit.

## Can I use the generated audio commercially?

Yes, with an appropriate license.

## Why are there two voices?

Voice blending creates more natural and expressive results.

## Is there a generation limit?

No. Generation is unlimited with an active license.
